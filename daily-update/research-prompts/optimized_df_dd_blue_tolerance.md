# Vetted Prompt: DF and DD willingness to tolerate a blue government

Date: **31 March 2026**

## Research question

How realistic is it that **Dansk Folkeparti (DF)** and **Danmarksdemokraterne (DD)** would let a **V-led government pass that includes Moderaterne**, without DF or DD being in government themselves?

The key issue is not generic blue sympathy. The key issue is whether DF and DD would:
1. let such a government take office,
2. abstain or tolerate it in an initial confidence situation,
3. do so even if Moderaterne is included,
4. do so without obtaining major concessions.

## Scope

Cover the full **post-election period from 24 March 2026 onward**.

Search:
- DR
- TV2
- Altinget
- Information
- Berlingske
- Politiken
- Jyllands-Posten
- Ritzau
- party press statements
- party social media

## Definitions

Treat **tolerance** as willingness to let a government take office or survive an initial confidence vote without joining it.

Distinguish clearly between:
- tolerating a government once
- abstaining tactically
- offering durable parliamentary support
- supporting budgets/finance acts
- joining government

Do not blur these together.

## Required workflow

### Step 1: Build an evidence ledger before drafting
Extract **all direct quotes and attributed statements** relevant to:
- DF willingness to tolerate a blue government
- DD willingness to tolerate a blue government
- DF view of Moderaterne / Lars Løkke Rasmussen
- DD view of Moderaterne / Lars Løkke Rasmussen
- conditions or demands for tolerance
- statements suggesting blocking behavior
- statements suggesting tolerance is impossible, unlikely, conditional, or easy

For each item, record:
- exact date
- source
- actor
- party
- exact quote or tight paraphrase if no quote exists
- whether it is direct evidence on tolerance
- whether it is specifically about **M-inclusive** blue government
- whether it indicates support, opposition, conditions, or ambiguity

### Step 2: Separate evidence types
Keep these categories separate:
1. direct post-election statements
2. attributed post-election reporting
3. historical precedent from 2022–2025
4. commentator analysis

Do not let commentary substitute for direct evidence.

### Step 3: Search for both confirming and disconfirming evidence
Do not assume the current model is wrong.
Search equally for:
- evidence that DF/DD would tolerate a V-led M-inclusive government
- evidence that DF/DD would refuse or heavily condition such tolerance

### Step 4: Explicitly audit for null findings
For each sub-question below, report either:
- evidence found, or
- **no evidence found**

Do not leave any sub-question implicitly unanswered.

## What to look for

### 1. DF post-election tolerance posture
- Has Morten Messerschmidt said anything after 24 March about tolerating a blue government DF is not part of?
- Has DF set explicit conditions or demands?
- Has DF suggested it would block such a government?
- Has DF said anything specifically about tolerating a government that includes Moderaterne or Løkke?

### 2. DD post-election tolerance posture
- Has Inger Støjberg said anything after 24 March about tolerating a blue government DD is not part of?
- Has DD set explicit conditions or demands?
- Has DD suggested it would block such a government?
- Has DD said anything specifically about tolerating a government that includes Moderaterne or Løkke?

### 3. DF–M and DD–M relationship dynamics
- Any evidence of personal or political hostility between Messerschmidt/Støjberg and Løkke?
- Any evidence of practical cooperation or openness?
- Historical precedent from 2022–2025 on M-inclusive governments or parliamentary support

### 4. The “free tolerance” question
- Is there evidence DF or DD would tolerate such a government without major concessions?
- Or does available evidence imply immigration/EU/welfare demands that could clash with M’s participation?

### 5. Strategic incentives to block
- Any evidence DF or DD would prefer to block blue to improve leverage?
- Any evidence they prefer opposition, new elections, or a different formation route?
- Any evidence competition between DF and DD reduces willingness to cooperate on tolerance?

### 6. Commentator analysis
- What do serious commentators say specifically about DF/DD tolerance realism?
- If they say blue chances are near zero, is that because of DF/DD tolerance, M’s orientation, BP fallout, or something else?

## What not to do

- Do not speculate beyond the sources.
- Do not propose parameter values.
- Do not treat commentary as a substitute for direct evidence.
- Do not collapse generic blue-bloc friendliness into willingness to tolerate an **M-inclusive** government.
- Do not omit exact dates.
- Do not omit exact quotes when available.
